Daun Jung

Hello! I am Daun Jung.

I received my bachelor’s degree from Yonsei University, where I double-majored in Library and Information Science and Computer Science, and completed an interdisciplinary program in Cognitive Science. My undergraduate studies were fully supported by the ILJU Foundation.

During my studies, I participated in international academic programs, including a summer school at the University of Leeds and an exchange program at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, where I studied Brain and Psychological Sciences.

I began a combined bachelor’s–master’s program in my third year and am currently pursuing a master’s degree in Cognitive Science, where I was selected as a university scholar and receive full financial support.

For more details, please see my Curriculum Vitae (CV).
You can also find me on LinkedIn and Google Scholar.
